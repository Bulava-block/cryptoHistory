HOW IT WORKS

COLLECT DIGITAL PIECES OF ART

Each collection consists of cards
Some of the cards can be bought
Some of them you will need to forge
(or buy from other collectors that have already forged them)
Each card has capacity and charges
Charges are to forge other cards
Every time a card is forged the card(cards)
that forged it lose 1 charges
Every card is forged by charges for this specific card

HOW TO FORGE A CARD

Let's say CardA+CardB=CardC:
A+B=C
A, B and C will be picked by the system
When the system picks cards A and B, any card
that is called A or B will be given a number of charges to forge a card C(not any card)

HOW TO DESTROY A CARD

In order to destroy a card you need to own 2 cards of the same name.
For example:
You own A1 and A3
in this case you can destroy either A1 or A3
By doing that you increase the value of the cards "A" because now
the number of cards A is lower
Capacity of the destroyed card along with all the remaining charges
will be added the card that's left

How it Works

Collect digital pieces of art

Each collection consists of cards.
Some of the cards can be bought,
some of them you will need to forge
(or buy from other collectors that have already created them)

how to create a forge

Let's say you have 2 collections of cards:
A, B, C and X, Y, Z.
Let's say there is only 5 collections A, B, C and 5 collections X, Y, Z.
Every card will have a number. That means:
A1, B1, C1
A2, B2, C2
etc....
Let's say there is a card "K" that requares "A" and "Z" in order to be forged
K=A+Z
So all you have to do is combine card "A" and card "Z" to forge card "K"
But....
Since we have 5 collections of ABC and 5 collections of XYZ the card "K" can only be forged 5 times
and there is no way to forge more.
The conditions to create a card are:
The cards that you pair together should not be have paired previously otherwise you won't forge a card
K=A1+Z1;
K=A1+Z5;
etc..
Let's say some other collector collects A1 and Z5 after you've used them already.
Now this collector want's to do the same,
but it will not work.

How to destroy cards

In order to destroy a card you need to own 2 cards of the same name.
For example:
You own A1 and A3;
in this case you can destroy either A1 or A3;
by doing that you increase the value of the cards "A" because now there is fewer of them in the system
and if they ever have to be used other for some other collections
other collectors will have to deal with the ones that own "A"
